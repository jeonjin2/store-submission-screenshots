# Mistakes To Avoid

The authoritative failure-mode list lives in `references/mistakes.md`.

Most important rules:

- Do not use wrong-brand, competitor, or store-listing screenshots as final assets.
- Do not invent extra screenshots when the user provides too few source screens.
- Do not assume explanation copy when the screen has no useful visual/textual clues.
- Do not make mockup/tilted layouts the default.
- Do not skip the `1024 x 500` featured graphic when Google Play is included.
