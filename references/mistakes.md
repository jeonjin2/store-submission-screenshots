# Mistakes To Avoid

- Do not treat a wrong-brand screenshot as acceptable by only changing visible text.
- Do not use `MemoriQ` screenshots for `OrderiQ` final assets.
- Do not use App Store search result screenshots as final submission assets.
- Do not put a standalone logo/icon into a promo image after the user asks to remove it.
- Do not spend time generating new app screenshots when the user has already placed the desired originals in Downloads for the current task.
- Do not invent extra screenshots when the user provided too few source screens. Warn and generate only what is available.
- Do not skip the featured graphic when Google Play is included; generate it or report why it cannot be generated.
- Do not assume explanation copy when a screenshot has no useful visual/textual clues. Ask for a short phrase.
- Do not keep asking for confirmation on reversible local asset generation.
- Do not make tilted/mockup layouts the default.
- Do not apply iPhone frame, notch, or tilted mockup rules to Google Play-including outputs by default.
- Do not apply iPhone mockup tilt rules to iPad/tablet outputs.
- Do not pick low-contrast background/text colors just because they match the app's brand color.
- Do not over-decorate explanation copy.
